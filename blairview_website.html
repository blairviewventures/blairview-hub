The diagnostic

Every team has a theory. Almost none of them are right.


The experience gap — the distance between what a team believes they shipped and what a user actually encounters — is the most expensive problem in consumer product. It compounds quietly. It shows up in churn, in NPS, in growth that stalls without a clear cause.

That gap is diagnosable. Not with a framework applied generically — with a specific, evidence-based finding about what is actually happening in your product and a prioritized path to closing it.

  • 01 Retention metrics that don't respond to product changes
  • 02 A launch that underperformed and produced no consensus on why
  • 03 A second product surface that's quietly eroding trust in the first
  • 04 An AI feature with adoption numbers that don't match internal confidence
  • 05 A product-market fit narrative that diverges from what users actually do
"The moment users cross from useful to indispensable is specific, fragile, and almost always invisible to the team that built it."

Engagements

Every engagement begins with a diagnosis. Not an assumption.

The right structure depends on where the problem sits and how much runway exists to address it. The conversation that determines fit comes before any scope is discussed.

01
Product Risk Brief
A focused engagement identifying the top experience risks in your product before they become revenue problems. Specific findings — no generalities.
5–7 days
02
Revenue Leak Audit
Front-to-back audit of where the consumer experience is actively costing revenue. Deliverable: a prioritized roadmap with financial impact attached to each finding.
14 days · Fixed fee
03
90-Day CX Sprint
Full embedded engagement — audit, roadmap, prioritization, and execution support. For organizations that cannot afford the cost of a prolonged diagnostic phase.
90 days
04
Consumer Launch Advisory
Structured advisory for companies bringing a first major consumer-facing product to market. HBO GO launched before streaming had a definition. That experience informs this work directly.
30 or 60 days
05
AI Product Advisory
For any company building an AI-powered user-facing product. The trust, adoption, and experience architecture problems are consistent across industries. The credential here is a production system, not a pilot.
30 days
06
Interview Coaching
For senior product managers pursuing VP, CPO, or Director roles at tier-one companies. Built from direct experience evaluating candidates at that level.
