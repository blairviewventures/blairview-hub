The problem I solve

Something is wrong with your customer experience. Everyone has a theory. Nobody has a diagnosis.

Your team built what the roadmap said to build. The product works. But users aren't converting the way they should. Retention is softer than the model projected. The NPS number doesn't move.

The gap between what your team thinks they built and what users actually feel is bigger than anyone expects. It is specific, it is findable, and it is fixable.

  • Churn that engineering can't explain and product can't prioritize
  • A launch that underperformed and nobody agrees why
  • A product-market fit story that doesn't match what users actually do
  • A second product surface that's cannibalizing the first
  • An AI feature your users don't trust yet
